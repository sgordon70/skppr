Questions

When do I pay?
Payment is collected after your site is reviewed and approved. Nothing is charged at submission. We'll send you a payment link once we've confirmed your site is a good fit.
What makes a site a good fit for Skppr?
Skppr users are curious, engaged, and looking for something genuinely interesting. Sites that perform best are content-rich, distinctive, and give users a reason to explore. We don't accept spam, thin affiliate sites, or anything that would embarrass a curious person for landing on it.
How many impressions will I get?
We're transparent: Skppr is growing and we don't promise specific impression numbers. What we do promise is that every impression is a real human who chose to discover something new — not a bot, not a passive scroll.
What's the difference between Basic and Sponsored?
Basic puts you in the rotation alongside thousands of other sites — users land on you when the algorithm picks your category. Sponsored gives you a dedicated card that appears every 5th skip with your custom headline, description, and call to action. Much higher guaranteed visibility.
Can I cancel?
Listings are annual. If you're not happy within the first 30 days we'll refund you, no questions asked.
